What it meant to me

Now for the important part and the whole reason of making this website in the first place, what it meant to me. At a first glance anyone would look at it for a moment and mostly think that it is just pretty, that's what I did at first too, but then after a while I started to think about it, what does it mean to me? And then thats when I noticed it, on the top right corner of the canvas I noticed that the sky was visible, but on teh top left corner had no sky and was blocked by the flowers. Then as I looked at the bottom of both of the sides I also noticed that on the left side it was beautiful and the grass was as green as it can be, however on teh right side I saw that the grass was green at first but then got drier afterwards. So to me I felt like it was portraying freedom. The right side of the painting was teh freedom side since the sky was visible but just barely, but teh left side was teh side where there was no freedom. I came to this conclusion because to me I felt like the left side was showing how the road is pretty but you won't gain freedom if you stay in that comfort zone. On the right side the path looks pretty good at first but then gets harder as you progress increasingly into freedom, or the sky. Like if you stay in your comfort zone then it will look good but you will never realy have access to that freedom. On the right side if you take the path that doesn't look pretty but if you keep going, then that freedom will soon be yours.No matter how the path looks at teh end it will all be worth it when you have truly gained freedom.
